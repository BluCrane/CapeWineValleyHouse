Welcome

Welcome to Cape Wine Valley House. We offer luxury accommodation in Aurora, a tranquil suburb of Durbanville. The area is ideally located within the greater Cape Town Metro, with easy access to the world-renowned beaches, restaurants and wine estates of Cape Town and the surrounding Cape Winelands.

Our 62m² Premium Suite with its panoramic views from the large balcony sleeps two adults and two children and includes a lounge, a kitchenette and an en-suite bathroom.

The 30m² Luxury Suite on the ground floor can accommodate two adults and comes with a private patio, a lounge and an en-suite bathroom.

Both units have their own private barbecue facilities as well as access to our large swimming pool.

All of which makes Cape Wine Valley House the perfect guest house for both leisure and business travellers.

Map and Directions

Explore

Durbanville is a town in the Western Cape province of South Africa, part of the greater City of Cape Town metropolitan area. It is centrally situated, about 20 minutes' drive from Cape Town and provides easy access to the Cape Winelands.

Local attractions include:

  • Wine tasting at internationally renowned wine estates
  • Fine dining at award-winning restaurants
  • Various shopping malls and conference centres
  • Some of the most beautiful beaches in the world, several with coveted Blue Flag status
  • Adventure sports such as kite surfing, mountain biking and trail running
  • Hiking in a wide variety of botanical gardens, biospheres and nature reserves
  • Game viewing

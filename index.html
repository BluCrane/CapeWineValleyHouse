Welcome to Cape Wine Valley House. We offer exclusive accommodation in Aurora, a tranquil suburb of Durbanville. The area is ideally located within the greater Cape Town Metro, with easy access to the world-renowned beaches, restaurants and wine estates of Cape Town and the surrounding Cape Winelands.

Our 62m² Premium Suite with panoramic views from the large balcony sleeps two adults and two children and includes a lounge, a kitchenette and an en-suite bathroom.

The 30m² Luxury Suite on the ground floor can accommodate two adults and comes with a private patio, a lounge and an en-suite bathroom.

Both units have their own private barbecue facilities as well as access to our large swimming pool.
